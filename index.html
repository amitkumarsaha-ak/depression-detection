About Depression

Depression is a serious mental health condition that affects how a person feels, thinks, and behaves. It can lead to emotional and physical problems and reduce a person's ability to function in daily life.

People who are not depressed usually maintain emotional balance and can handle daily stress effectively. However, individuals with depression may experience persistent sadness, loss of interest, low energy, and difficulty concentrating.

Early detection and support can help improve mental health and overall well-being.
