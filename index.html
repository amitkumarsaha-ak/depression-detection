About Mental Health

Depression: Depression is a serious mental health condition that affects how a person feels, thinks, and behaves.

  • Persistent sadness or hopelessness
  • Loss of interest in activities
  • Low energy or fatigue
  • Sleep problems
  • Difficulty concentrating

Non-Depression (Healthy State): A mentally healthy person maintains emotional balance and can handle stress effectively.

  • Stable mood
  • Positive thinking
  • Good energy levels
  • Active lifestyle

Early awareness and support can help improve mental health and overall well-being.
